### 🧠 About Me

📱 I'm a **Flutter Developer** who builds clean, cross-platform mobile apps with **Dart**, solid state management, and a strong eye for UI/UX — I care as much about how an app *feels* as how it works.

🔌 I'm also an **Embedded Systems Engineer**, with hands-on experience in **bare-metal programming**, **PIC microcontrollers**, **C/Assembly**, and **Proteus simulation** — this background gives me a deep understanding of what's happening under the hood, from the hardware all the way up to the UI.

🛠️ Right now I'm sharpening my skills in **Flutter architecture (MVVM/Clean Architecture)**, **state management**, and **API integration**, while continuing to grow in **system design** and **IoT**.

⚡ I write clean, modular, well-structured code — whether it's a widget tree or a register-level driver.
